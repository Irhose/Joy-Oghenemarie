About Me

Hi, I'm Joy Oghenemarie. I translate complex ideas, emotions, and narratives into fluid, stylized 2D motion graphics.

With years of experience blending timing, visual poetry, and sound, I specialize in character-driven storytelling and abstract vector animations. I believe every motion frame should serve the story, creating a rhythmic dialogue between the characters on screen and the audience.
